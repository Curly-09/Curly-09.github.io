About
Who I am

Hi, I'm Sairaj Ambekar, a 17-year-old indie developer from Mumbai, India. I'm currently a 3rd year student at Vidyalankar Polytechnic and the founder of Sylix — a free-to-run native AI code editor built from scratch in Rust.

I built the whole thing solo: the native editor, the backend, the API layer, the model routing, and the verify loop. Most AI coding tools just write code. Sylix is built to understand the project, remember your rules, test what it writes, and actually finish the task.

I did a Fullstack Development Internship at Cloud Counselage Pvt. Ltd (May 2025 – June 2026) and I'm currently learning Machine Learning. I'm a weekly open-source contributor — I contribute to open source projects and side projects once a week.

I cold-DMed 100+ developers to figure out what a real AI editor should do, then built it.
